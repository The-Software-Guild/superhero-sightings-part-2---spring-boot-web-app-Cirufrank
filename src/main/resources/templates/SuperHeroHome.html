About Us

With the rising popularity of superhero movies, there has been a heightened awareness of superheroes in our midst. The frequency of Superhero (and Supervillain) sightings is increasing at an alarming rate. Given this development, the Hero Education and Relationship Organization (HERO) has asked our company to develop a database. This application lists the name, powers, organizations, and various locations of organizations and sightings of a Superhero or Supervillain.
